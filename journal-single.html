By Henry Bean

My passion is to create and bring visions to life.

In the Begining

My design career started several around 5-6 years ago, I've always had an interest in creating and the design field was the most suitable field for me to invest myself in.

I started off learning my career by investing my time and energy into creative graphics work, doing photoshop artwork and small scale stuff, later on enrolling myself in various design competitions.

After the first year of participating in competative design works, i excelled and started winning some competitions in random artowrk forums, which gave me the self esteem to think about moving towards making design work my main work field.

My working experience started off by making free artwork for people on different platforms, I did that untill I made a name for myself, then people started reaching out to me for paid work. I used to have a mindset that if something was really good, people would look for me and my work and eventually I'd start making some income from it, which did happen in the long run.

A few years in

After making some small income from small scale work I finally felt confident enough to start on taking some larger projects and I then moved on to the UI/UX Development fields/

I applied the same knowledge again, creating some free work and learning on the go, untill I had sufficient knowledge to start asking people for money. It tooke me several months untill I ahd my first paid project, which was a wordpress project.
